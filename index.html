Why Health & Fitness Are Important

Health and fitness improve physical strength, mental clarity, immunity, and emotional well-being. A healthy lifestyle keeps diseases away and improves confidence.

  • Boosts immunity and energy levels
  • Improves concentration and memory
  • Keeps the body fit and active
  • Supports mental and emotional health

Nutrition Fundamentals

A balanced diet is necessary for good health, growth, and energy.

Components of a Balanced Diet

  • Carbohydrates – main energy source
  • Proteins – muscle and tissue repair
  • Fats – energy storage and warmth
  • Vitamins – immunity and growth
  • Minerals – strong bones and teeth
  • Water – essential for survival

Water Intake Guide

  • Teenagers: 6–8 glasses daily
  • More water needed during exercise or summer

Exercise & Physical Activity

Regular exercise keeps the body strong, improves heart health, and boosts mood.

Types of Exercise

1. Strength Training

  • Push-ups, squats
  • Builds muscles and bones

2. Cardio

  • Running, walking, cycling
  • Improves stamina and heart health

3. Flexibility

  • Yoga, stretching
  • Reduces pain and stiffness

Yoga for Mind & Body

Yoga calms the mind, increases flexibility, and improves balance.

Benefits of Yoga

  • Reduces stress
  • Improves breathing
  • Enhances concentration
  • Improves posture

Mental Health & Well-being

Tips for Mental Wellness

  • Practice deep breathing
  • Talk to friends/family
  • Take breaks from study
  • Meditate daily

Sleep Hygiene

  • Sleep early, wake up early
  • Avoid mobile screens before sleeping
  • Keep room calm and quiet

BMI (Body Mass Index) Guide

BMI shows whether your weight is healthy for your height.

BMI Formula: Weight (kg) / Height (m²)

  • Underweight: Less than 18.5
  • Normal: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: 30+
